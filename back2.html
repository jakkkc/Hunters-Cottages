Our Story

In the heart of the Bukusu community, a hunter named Simiyu—a name given to children born during drought, symbolizing survival and resilience—dreamed of a peaceful retreat after his hunts. This vision gave birth to Hunters Paradise Cottages, where everyone, including those with disabilities, could find welcome and rest.

Inspired by the majestic animals of the wild, each cottage and facility carries a name and meaning drawn from nature. From Eswara, our conference room named after the agile antelope, to Etalangi, our luxurious lion-inspired VIP cottage, every detail tells a story of harmony with nature.

Today, we continue Simiyu's legacy of hospitality, offering a sanctuary where guests can connect with nature, celebrate traditions, and create lasting memories.
