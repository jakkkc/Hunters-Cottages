About Hunters Paradise Cottages

Founded by Simiyu, a hunter who needed a place to share his meals with family and friends, Hunters Paradise Cottages embodies warm hospitality, peaceful grounds, and uniquely themed accommodations. "Simiyu" is a special name in the Bukusu community given to children born during a drought—a reminder of survival and resilience.

Our cottages are ideal for families, small groups, retreats, and conferences. Enjoy our restaurant, the Duma bar, excursions to local attractions, and modern conferencing facilities.

Children policy: Children below 5 years sharing a room stay complimentary (maximum one child per room). Children 6–12 years in their own room pay 75% of the adult rate.

Our Accommodation

Each cottage is named after Kenya's majestic animals, reflecting their unique characteristics

Engwe — Leopard (Superior Room)

Our superior room with elegant finishes, private bathroom and balcony views. The leopard represents stealth and agility.

  • King or Twin bed options
  • En-suite bathroom with premium amenities
  • Private balcony with wilderness views
  • Free Wi-Fi & satellite TV
  • Tea/coffee making facilities

Kibeu — Rhino (Executive Cottage)

Executive cottage inspired by the rhino's quiet strength. Features generous living area and private outdoor space.

  • Separate living and sleeping areas
  • Private veranda with seating
  • Premium tea/coffee station
  • Enhanced bathroom amenities
  • Complimentary mineral water

Emboko — Buffalo (Deluxe Cottage)

Comfortable deluxe cottage representing the buffalo's strength and unity. Ideal for couples seeking extra space.

  • Spacious bedroom with sitting area
  • Private garden space
  • Complimentary breakfast served in-room
  • Enhanced storage space
  • Luxury bathrobes and slippers

Enjofu — Elephant (Family Cottage)

Family cottage that accommodates up to 4 people, inspired by the elephant's wisdom and protective nature.

  • Two separate bedrooms
  • Spacious living area
  • Baby cot available on request at no extra cost
  • Family-friendly amenities
  • Child safety features

Etalangi — Lion (VIP Cottage)

Our premium VIP cottage offering exclusive amenities and privacy, representing the lion's dominance and leadership.

  • Luxury fittings and finishes
  • Private lounge and dining area
  • Dedicated butler service
  • Premium minibar selection
  • Exclusive check-in/check-out

Facilities & Services

Ekwena Restaurant

Named after the crocodile, representing strength and patience. Enjoy local and international dishes served in a warm setting with attentive service.

Duma Bar

Named after the cheetah, representing speed and energy. Our bar offers a vibrant atmosphere for drinks and social evenings with friends and fellow travelers.

Eswara Conference Room

Named after the antelope, representing agility and awareness. Our conference facility accommodates up to 80 people with modern amenities.
