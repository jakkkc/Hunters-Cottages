About Hunters Paradise Cottages

Hunters Paradise Cottages was born from the dream of a generous hunter who wanted a peaceful place to gather with family and friends. Rooted in Bukusu traditions, each cottage is named after a powerful animal and designed to bring guests closer to nature while offering modern comforts and accessible facilities for all.

The cottages — Engwe (Leopard), Kibeu (Rhino), Emboko (Buffalo), Enjofu (Elephant) and Etalangi (Lion) — tell a story of strength, unity and hospitality. The property also includes comfortable communal spaces like Ekwena restaurant, Duma bar, a swimming pool and Eswara conference room.
